About Me

Hi all, my name is Sam. I am currently working as an Electrical Engineer in the Digital Grid division of Siemens Australia. I graduated from Griffith University on the Gold Coast and moved to Melbourne February of 2018. I am currently in a sales position and do not particularly enjoy it, I never saw myself as a salesman and it is definitely not where I saw my career heading as an Engineer. I always enjoyed my coding subjects at Uni, and I have gained an interest in the development of web applications specifically suited for the emerging IoT area of energy distribution. I believe this course will give me the necessary skills to follow this area of interest.

In my spare time I like to go to the gym, play video games, read and also write. I am working on a novel that will be put on hold until I finish this course. I have a fat ginger cat called Fatty who is pictured to the left. I love to travel, especially to places where there is snow. I have been to Japan 3 times now to snowboard and each trip has been better than the last. I try and snowboard any chance I get. I will be going to Austria at the end of this year to snowboard the Alps which I am super keen for.
